Get acquainted with Machakos

Machakos also called Masaku is a town in Kenya, 63 km(39mi) southeast of Nairobi. It is the capital of the Machakos County, Kenya. MachakosCounty had a population of 1,421,932 as of 2019. People who live here are mostly of the Akamba ethinicity. Machakos is surrounded by hilly terrain, with a high number of family farms.
